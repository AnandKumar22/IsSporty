Who We Are?

Know about Us

At IsSporty, we want to solve the problem of connecting people with sports and sports with people.The market for sports engagement is highly fragmented leading to everyday hassle of finding the right players for the right venues and vice versa. Parents don’t know where to look for the best facilities and coaches, how to compare them, or even how much to pay for them. Sports academies and coaches don’t know how to utilize their resources and how much do they need to spend in order to efficiently manage their offerings.
Throughout the history of this business, people have been using the word of mouth marketing to make more informed decisions. Our mission at IsSporty is to contribute towards the development of a sustainable sports community with the help of our 3T principles (Trust, Transparency and Technology).
IsSporty is an one stop platform for all kind of sports services ranging from sports infrastructure booking, coaching, tournament and events participation, finding players and joining practice sessions. IsSporty helps business owners to leverage the power of technology for a more efficient, secure and sophisticated method for business.
